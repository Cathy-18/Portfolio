About Me

I'm Catherine Nixon, a dedicated and enthusiastic Computer Science student with a passion for merging technology and creativity. My journey in tech began when I discovered how coding could bring innovative ideas to life, and since then I’ve been eager to explore and build impactful projects.

I specialize in Python and Django for backend development and have hands-on experience through an internship with Facein Technologies Pvt Ltd. Alongside coding, I enjoy exploring AI, UI/UX, and digital product design. I love turning complex problems into simple, intuitive solutions that enhance user experiences.

Beyond technology, I’m deeply interested in fashion design and crocheting. I’ve coordinated and participated in creative events like the ‘Safer is Sexy’ campaign, the Rangwaali Holi event, and showcased a couture outfit inspired by nature at Brahma’24. These experiences help me stay creative and bring fresh perspectives into my work.

I’m excited to keep learning, collaborating, and building projects that bridge the gap between tech and creativity!
